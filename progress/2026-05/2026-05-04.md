## Per-model throughput audit (27B / 31B / 122B-A10B)

User asked why Qwen3.6-27B Q4_K_M, SuperGemma4-31B Q4_K_M, and Qwen3.5-122B-A10B Q4_K_M run at single-digit t/s on EPYC despite ~460 GB/s aggregate BW. Mental model under audit was "BW = 460 GB/s, so dense 27-31B should easily hit double-digit decode."

### Finding: the gap is mostly physics + arch, not config

Pulled v5 canonical numbers from `epyc-inference-research/benchmarks/results/runs/20260430_151713/`:

| Model | Quant | Weights | Theoretical (peak BW) | Realistic (24% util Ōēł 110 GB/s) | v5 measured | Verdict |

| Qwen3.6-27B (dense hybrid GDN+Attn) | Q4_K_M | 16.3 GB | ~28 t/s | ~7.4 t/s | **6.65 t/s** | at ceiling |
| Qwen3.6-27B | Q8_0 | 27.0 GB | ~17 t/s | ~4.1 t/s | **4.54 t/s** | at ceiling |

| Qwen3.5-122B-A10B (MoE) | Q4_K_M | 69 GB / ~10B active | varies | varies | **4.3 t/s/instance** | unmeasured under canonical (probe queued) |

The 460 GB/s ceiling assumes 100% BW utilization. Per `qwen36-27b-cpu-feasibility.md:44` and `cpu-shape-specialized-gemv-decode.md:165`, dense decode on EPYC 9655 actually realizes ~24% (110 GB/s effective) due to barrier overhead ├Ś hybrid op count + scalar fallback paths + KV traffic. perf-stat shows 0.17 IPC and 96.6% backend-stalled-on-memory at 96 threads ŌĆö decisive DRAM-bound confirmation.

For the 27B/31B at single-digit t/s the answer is: **at canonical ceiling, not a config bug**. For 122B-A10B (4.3 t/s) the v5 deployment draft has the role under `todo_or_undecided` (line 214) ŌĆö never had a clean per-arch canonical measurement. Plausible 2├Ś single-instance headroom, queued for Probe B.

Initial framing of "OMP env stack + GGML_NUMA_WEIGHTS not in production = 3-4├Ś hit" was corrected mid-session: those levers compound on **MoE** workloads, but per the v5 deployment matrix `dense_q8` and `dense_q4` rows explicitly mark all CPU1/mbind-off probes as **net-negative** for the 27B/31B class.

## Ring-mini structural fix (reasoning_budget=0) + production-stack survey

### Context
After May 2 ring-mini benchmarks showed 4 empty responses (70% pass rate) and May 4 attempt with `max_tokens_multiplier: 4` made it WORSE (7 empties, 65.6%), root-cause analysis confirmed Lightning Attention's stuck-in-think failure mode on multi-constraint enumeration problems.

### Root-cause finding (research/deep-dives/ring-mini-stuck-in-think-failure-mode.md)
All 7 May-4 empties shared identical signature: `completion_tokens=4096`, ~52s wall, `len(response)=0`, harness-stripped unclosed `<think>` block. Ring-mini's 16-of-20 linear-attention layers lose early-context recall as think trace grows past ~2K tokens; the 4 softmax layers can't bridge. Multi-constraint enumeration problems (planning, scheduling, multi-agent coordination, reasoning traps, Fermi estimation) require backreferencing early constraints during candidate-verification ŌĆö exactly the operation Lightning Attention can't do reliably.

Server-side llama-server flag forces immediate `</think>` after the template's `<think>` open. Already wired through executor.py:302-303 via `reasoning_budget` registry field. Set on `ring_mini_linear_q4km` in research-registry.
