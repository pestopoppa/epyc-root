### Response: Deterministic Testing

Created `test-repack-parallel.cpp` that validates:
- **Byte-identical output** between 1-thread and N-thread execution
- **All parallelized repack functions**: Q4_0, Q4_K, Q2_K, IQ4_NL
- **Multiple thread configurations**: 1v1, 1v2, 1v4, 1v8, 1v16, 1vmax, 2v4, 4v8

**Key insight**: Repacking is deterministic (no floating-point approximation). Any race condition or indexing error causes byte-level differences, making the test robust.
