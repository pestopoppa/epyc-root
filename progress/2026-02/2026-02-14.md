- LocalLlamaTeacher excluded — distillation is API-only (Claude Opus 4.6 + Codex gpt-5.3)

### Gap 2: Debugger Integration

### Gap 3: Replay Integration
- **seed_specialist_routing.py**: `--debug-replay` section tries `SkillAwareReplayEngine` with `SkillBankConfig` before falling back to standard `ReplayEngine`. Prints skill metrics (total retrieved, coverage, avg/step)

### Gap 4: OutcomeTracker Wiring

- Records `skill_id × task_id × success` after reward injection for each role result
