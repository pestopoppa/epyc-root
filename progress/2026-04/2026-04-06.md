### LangGraph Migration Phase 2 — Reducer Fix + Dual-Run Validation

Fixed a **critical reducer semantics bug** in Phase 1: `_state_update()` returned full list contents for `operator.add` fields, causing exponential list growth when LangGraph's reducers concatenated them with existing state.

- `nodes.py`: `_build_ctx()` returns 4-tuple with snapshot lengths. `_state_update()` and `_handle_end()` trim append-reducer fields to deltas. All 7 node functions updated.
