# Research Deep-Dives

105 long-form analyses of individual papers, repositories, and techniques. Each one is written when a topic warrants more than a triage entry — typically a few pages with claims, evidence, applicability to the EPYC stack, and (where relevant) falsifiable predictions.

These are the raw substrate of the [Topics](../topics/index.md) syntheses. If a topic article references "Source: deep-dives/foo.md," this is the directory it lives in.

Browse by title in the nav on the left, or use the search box (top right) to jump to a specific paper or technique. All deep-dives are hand-authored — they're not auto-generated summaries.

For the curated narrative reading order, see [Stories](../stories/index.md). For the production-stack walkthroughs, see [Subsystems](../subsystems/index.md).
