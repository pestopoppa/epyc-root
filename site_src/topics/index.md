# Topics

30 compiled articles synthesizing the project's research across every domain we've worked in. Each article pulls together findings from research deep-dives, completed handoffs, progress logs, and child-repo documentation into a single navigable reference. Citations preserved.

This is the **topic** view. For the **subsystem** view (how each piece of the production stack is built), see [Subsystems](../subsystems/index.md). For long-form per-paper analyses, see [Deep-Dives](../deep-dives/index.md).

## How articles are organized

Articles are grouped by domain:

- **Core inference optimization** — speculative decoding, MoE, KV cache, quantization, hardware
- **Serving & systems** — inference serving, local inference, chat templates
- **Routing & evaluation** — cost-aware routing, routing intelligence, benchmark methodology
- **Agent & architecture** — agent architecture, autonomous research, memory-augmented models
- **Context & compression** — context management, context extension, SSM & hybrid architectures
- **Training & distillation** — training & distillation, reinforcement learning
- **Multimodal & domain** — multimodal, document processing, formal verification
- **Knowledge & retrieval** — search & retrieval, knowledge management, RAG alternatives, tool implementation
- **Research & analysis** — LLM prompting, mechanistic interpretability

Use the navigation on the left to browse, or the search box (top right) to jump to a topic. Each article ends with a **Source References** section linking back to its deep-dives, handoffs, and progress entries.
